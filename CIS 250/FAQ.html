Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Adoption

1. How do I adopt a pet?

You can adopt a pet by visiting our adoption page and filling out the application form.

2. What is the adoption fee?

The adoption fee varies depending on the pet. This fee helps cover vaccinations and spaying/neutering costs.

3. Can I adopt more than one pet?

Yes! We encourage adopting multiple pets if you are able to provide them with a loving home.

4. What if I need to return my adopted pet?

If you need to return your pet, please contact us immediately. We want to ensure every animal finds a safe and suitable home.
